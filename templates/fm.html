Frequency Modulation


We’re almost ready to patch our synth but before we do that we have to talk about what FM synthesis is. FM synthesis is when one oscillator modulates another oscillator at a variety of rates to create an infinite amount of timbral variation. This can be seen here:

○ Sine Wave Patch Point
This outputs a sine wave signal. Use this to connect the oscillator to the VCA.
▹ Click sine wave patch point
▹ Click VCA input patch point
○ Frequency Knob
This will adjust the frequency of the sine wave. Turn this dial to hear and see its effect.
